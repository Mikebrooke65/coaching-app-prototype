---LESSON START---
TITLE: Shooting with Power
AGE GROUP: U12
SKILL CATEGORY: Shooting
LEVEL: Intermediate
DURATION: 60
DESCRIPTION: Develop powerful shooting technique using proper body mechanics and follow-through. Focus on striking through the ball and generating power from the hips.

OBJECTIVES:
- Strike ball with laces for power
- Use proper approach angle
- Generate power from hips and core
- Follow through towards target

SESSION 1: Warm-Up & Technical Fundamentals (15 min)
Description: Static shooting practice focusing on technique without pressure
Equipment: Balls (1 per player), Cones (10), Goals (2)
Coaching Points:
- Plant foot beside ball
- Strike with laces
- Knee over ball
- Follow through

SESSION 2: Skill Introduction (15 min)
Description: Shooting from different angles and distances
Equipment: Balls (10), Cones (20), Goals (2), Mannequins (3)
Coaching Points:
- Approach at angle
- Head down on contact
- Strike through center of ball
- Power from hips

SESSION 3: Progressive Skill Development (15 min)
Description: Shooting under pressure with defenders
Equipment: Balls (8), Cones (16), Bibs (2 colors), Goals (2)
Coaching Points:
- Quick setup touch
- Shoot early when possible
- Stay balanced
- Be confident

SESSION 4: Small-Sided Game Application (15 min)
Description: Small-sided game with shooting zones
Equipment: Balls (3), Cones (12), Bibs (2 colors), Goals (2)
Coaching Points:
- Recognize shooting opportunities
- Get shot off quickly
- Shoot on sight
- Follow up rebounds
---LESSON END---
